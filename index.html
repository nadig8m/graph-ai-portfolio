About me

Medicine, genetics, and reproducible research.

My professional interests combine clinical genomics, molecular biology, population-aware interpretation of genetic data, and clear scientific communication.

01Training

About the course

No-code AI tools for responsible task automation.

The GRAPH Courses bootcamp combines live workshops, help sessions, personalized feedback, project coaching, a private community, and a final showcase.

An 8-week guided cohort for work and research productivity.

The course emphasizes responsible AI use across writing, data analysis, literature review, chatbots, automation, communication, and final project development.

  • Weekly live workshops and help sessions
  • Hands-on assignments with personalized feedback
  • Capstone project coaching and final showcase
  • Shareable certificate and tutorial library access
Module 1

Websites, documents & presentations

LLM foundations, Markdown, LaTeX, HTML, simple research websites, and AI-assisted scientific communication.

Module 2

AI-automated data analysis

Responsible data analysis, dashboards, file processing, reports, visualizations, and spreadsheet automations.

Module 3

Research & text analysis

Literature search, text classification, sentiment analysis, knowledge maps, summarization, and synthesis workflows.

Module 4

Custom GPTs and chatbots

Chat architecture, context windows, RAG systems, custom GPTs, Botpress, and research knowledge bases.

Module 5

AI for task automation

Agent-based systems, email response automations, complex workflow agents, and browser-control tools.

Module 6

Communication and outreach

Research summaries for different audiences, scientific visuals, video abstracts, and content scheduling.

Module 7

Capstone project & showcase

Project refinement, peer feedback, final presentations, and responsible AI practice.

Assessment
